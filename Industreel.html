America's Heartland has been hallowed out and now the only thing that remains are the archological relics of a long lost past. Take the 60 east till the tall buildings and housing is long in the rear view mirror and wander off the freeway and its there, the sights of a once great industrial mecca now laying mostly fallow. Its a strange and surreal mash up of building and industrial plants that is devoid of people by day though the signs of their presence is unavoidable. Use caution after dark.
